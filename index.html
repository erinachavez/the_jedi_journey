My Design Process

I would say that my inspiration for building this game is more than a bit obvious.

Growing up, I surprisingly didn't watch or engage with Star Wars really at all. However, less than a year ago, I decided that it was high time for me to catch up on this large pop culture phenonmenon. Not only did I catch up, but I became a hard-core fan. The Last Jedi, released just about 2 months ago, was easily one of my favorite movies of the whole Star Wars series, and I am managing to still be fan-girling over it now. Thus, I chose to dedicate this game to my newfound infatuation.

I was pretty stumped as to what to do at first because my ideas were consistently proving to be beyond my skill level with JavaScript's p5 library. Also, I was limited by both the assignment's requirements, and time. I did however know that I wanted the game to be in an arcade-adventure style, and stuck with that core throughout my trials and errors.

Unfortunately, this game is still unfinished. I know exactly how I want this game to continue, and do plan on finishing it. However for now, since I now believe I've fullfilled the assignment's requirements, and am already late turning this in (oops), I'll have to complete this at another time.

Thank you for playing and may the force be with you!
